Configuration

Enter your Spotify Developer Credentials to allow this app to access your accounts.

Where to find these?
  1. Log in to Spotify Dashboard.
  2. Create an App named "TransferMySong".
  3. Add these Redirect URIs in settings:
    {{ request.host_url.replace('localhost', '127.0.0.1') }}callback/source
    {{ request.host_url.replace('localhost', '127.0.0.1') }}callback/dest
